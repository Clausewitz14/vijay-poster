How to keep safe from Covid-19

~By Vijay Narayanan, a Prograd Junior

Wash hands regularly

Regular hand wash for 20 seconds width soap or sanitizer will help you avoid germs

Cover your mouth and nose

Cover your mouth and nose in public. Wearing a mask is recommended

Stay home
Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or going to crowded places. Staying home is the best.

Eat Healthy

Avoid junk food. Eating healthy, homemade food is the safest

Stay calm

Do exercise and yoga to stay chill and calm.

BE AWARE.BE SAFE.
